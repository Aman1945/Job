## AUDIT PREPARATION TIMELINE

For each audit, follow this timeline:

| Week | Activity | Responsible |
|------|----------|-------------|
| -4 | Audit planning, scope definition | Lead Auditor |
| -3 | Assign audit team, prepare checklist | Lead Auditor |
| -2 | Send audit plan to auditees | Management Rep |
| -1 | Document review, finalize checklist | Audit Team |
| 0 | Conduct audit | Audit Team |
| +1 | Prepare and distribute audit report | Lead Auditor |
| +2-4 | Corrective action planning | Auditees |
| +4-8 | Implement corrective actions | Auditees |
| +8 | Verify corrective actions | Lead Auditor |
